About us...

PPL Solutions was founded on 7 September 2024, by Wietsegaming and MaleklecoCZE with the goal to make a succesful and friendly VTC where people from all over the world can hangout and drive with eachother.

What do we offer?

Friendly Staff

We have a small but extremely friendly staff team that can help you with any problem you might face while driving for us.

Save-Edits

Our drivers have access to a wide variety of custom-made save-edits, including full-sets of trucks, custom parts, and much more.

Custom Mods

We also offer some exclusive PPL Solutions mods to use while playing singleplayer or convoys mode.
